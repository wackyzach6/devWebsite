Task 1.1 describe purpose of IT in modern business

Time saving - A lot of tasks are automated, sped up or cleaner with the use of IT such as the use of online forms for applications being sent in to a company rather than by post which requires a lot of manual work to sort and process the many applicants .

Wider reach of communication - With IT businesses are able to contact a large amount of people through emails or through online forms or chat services on their websites.

Increasing skills - Some technology such as Virtual Reality[1] has helped surgeons by live-streaming operations with 360 degree perspectives so people around the world could watch and learn how the procedure is performed, this is more efficient than the normal way as instead of just a few trainees watching the operation thousands can watch and learn.

As with any task there is always risks that will be a detriment to the projects success, some of these risks will be known to us that we will know how to deal with, some will be known but that we do not know how to deal with them, some will be unknown but through planning we can be ready to deal with these issues that pop up.

Some examples of how GDPR effects digitals projects are- Emails for deals and other information, the form is defaulted to no or is blank and people have to manually opt in to the service Individuals can opt out of the company having their data and have to dispose of the data they store Companies can only store what data is absolutely necessary and only for however long the data is necessary for its intended purpose and then disposed of.
